Nutrient-Rich Palak Paneer

Cubes of Indian cheese (paneer) submerged in a vibrant, creamy spinach puree seasoned with aromatic Indian spices. This version maximizes the natural nutrients of the spinach and uses a light cream alternative for richness without heaviness.

Protein: High Prep: 15 min Cook: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer, diced
  • 500g Fresh Spinach
  • 1 large Onion, chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Low-fat Yogurt or cashew paste (for creaminess)
  • Spices: Turmeric, Cumin, Garam Masala

Preparation Steps

  1. **Blanch Spinach:** Blanch the spinach leaves in hot water for 2 minutes, then immediately transfer to ice water to retain color. Blend to a smooth puree.
  2. **Sauté Base:** Lightly sauté chopped onions until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute. Add tomato puree and spices, cooking until oil separates.
  3. **Combine:** Stir the spinach puree into the spiced base. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. **Finish:** Gently fold in the diced paneer and low-fat yogurt/cashew paste. Simmer for 3 minutes (do not boil). Serve hot with Roti or Brown Rice.
